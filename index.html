About the Project

Drekoria is a fantasy world crafted for immersive storytelling, audio adventures, and community-driven creativity. It blends ancient myth with original lore, offering listeners and readers a new kind of journey — beyond the borders of maps, kingdoms, and time itself.

This project is developed by Ivaylo Kolev and a small but dedicated team of creatives, voice talents, and fantasy enthusiasts. From the narrative and lore to the voice-overs and visual elements, every part of Drekoria is hand-built with passion and purpose.
