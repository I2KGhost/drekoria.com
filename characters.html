Characters of Drekoria

Meet the key figures whose choices shape the fate of the known world.

Vatah

A boy raised in the wild, now caught between destiny and survival. Trained in the fortress of Velstone.

Dalemir

A hunter with a mysterious past. Stoic, wise, and the one who raised Vatah.

Lev

A young blacksmith’s apprentice and Vatah’s only true friend. Loyal and brave.

Marek

Ambitious and proud. Once an ally, now a rival within the brotherhood.
